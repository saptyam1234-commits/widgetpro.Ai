Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with ChatWidgetPro. If you are not happy with our service, we are here to help. Please read our refund policy carefully.

2. Free Trial

All new accounts receive a 7-day free trial. No payment is required during the trial period. You will only be charged if you choose to upgrade to a paid plan.

💡 We recommend using the free trial to evaluate the service before subscribing.

3. Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund under the following conditions:

  • Request submitted within 7 days of the initial payment
  • Service was not used significantly (under 100 messages sent)
  • A technical issue on our end prevented you from using the service
  • Duplicate or accidental payment was made

4. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Refund requests after 7 days of payment
  • Accounts with significant usage during the billing period
  • Cancellations mid-cycle (service continues until period ends)
  • Free trial accounts (no charge was made)

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact our support team with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Payment ID or transaction reference
  • Reason for the refund request

📧 support@chatwidgetpro.com

6. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card provider.

7. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. No further charges will be made after cancellation.
